R A I N B O W COLORS

R RED is known as the hue of intense emotions, including anger, self-sacrifice, danger, and heat, as well as love, passion, and sexuality.
O Orange is a warm, vibrant hue that radiates warmth and energy. It blends the energy of red with the cheerfulness of yellow, the two colors it lies between on the color wheel.
Y Yellow, which is associated with sunshine and sunflowers, is optimistic, fun, and happy. It is also connected to intellect and mental clarity.
G Green is a color that symbolizes growth and renewal in nature. It also has a connection to wealth, prestige, and greed.
B Blue has historically been connected with royalty, art, the military, business, and nature, making it a versatile color.
I Indigo represents devotion, wisdom, justice, and superior knowledge. It is also considered spiritual because it is linked to intuition and what cannot be seen.
V Violet, like blue, evokes feelings of intelligence and confidence. It is also related to individuality and creativity.
